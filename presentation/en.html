Ohm's law

  • U = Volt
  • I = Ampere
  • R = Ohm
  • Arduino output pin ~= 5V
  • LED ~= 0.020 or 20mA and 3V
  • 5V - 3V = 2V; 2V / 0.02A = 100Ω
  • Use at least 100Ω resistor
